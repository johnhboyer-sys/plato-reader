Support the Plato Reader

The Plato Reader is an ad-free parallel Greek–English edition of Plato’s dialogues — with click-to-parse morphology, dictionary entries, and exact Stephanus citation. It is free to read and always will be.

It is built and maintained by one person, and runs on no advertising and no paywall. A contribution helps defray development costs and keeps the reader free and open for students, teachers, and readers everywhere.

  • Hosting and the tools that keep the site fast and ad-free.
  • Aligning more public-domain translations to the Greek, line by line.
  • Adding works, glossaries, and reading aids across the corpus.
