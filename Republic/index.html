Republic

Πολιτεία

Traditionally dated to Plato’s middle period.

Socrates constructs the just city in speech to find justice in the soul — the central work of the corpus.

This edition presents the original Greek (Burnet (OCT, 1902)) side by side with an English translation (Paul Shorey) — with click-to-parse morphology, dictionary entries, and exact Stephanus citation. 10 books, 278 pages.
