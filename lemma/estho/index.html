Ag. 1597, Archipp. 20, Philippid. 9.5, Python 1.13, Call. Iamb. 1.270, Matro l.c. : sts. in later Prose, LXXLe. 17.10, 19.26, Plu. 2.101d codd. ; in Cos, Arch.f.Religionswiss. 10.402 (iii B. C.):—Pass., LXXLe. 17.13.
