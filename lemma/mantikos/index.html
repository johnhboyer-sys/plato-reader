Pol. 1274a28, etc.; more freq. ἡ μαντική alone, Hdt. 2.49, 4.68; ἡ . . μ. ἡ τοῦ δαιμονίου, of Socrates, Pl. Ap. 40a: in Poets without Art., A. Pr. 484, S.
