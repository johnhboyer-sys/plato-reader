τίτθη, ἡ, A. nurse, Ar. Eq. 716, Th. 609, Pl. R. 343a, Thphr. Char. 16.12.20.5, IG 2729.3, al. (iii B.C.), etc.; prop. wet-nurse, αἱ τ. καὶ αἱ τροφοί Plu. 2.3c, cf. Ptol.Asc.p.394H., Gal. 6.686; sts. confused with τήθη (q.v.); written τιθή in Hsch., τίθθη in Com. Adesp.Oxy.1825.8 (Pap. of v A.D.); τίθθη = matertera, Gloss. (i.e. confused with τηθίς). II. = τιτθός I, Arist. HA 587b17, 588a5, IG 22.1534.256.
