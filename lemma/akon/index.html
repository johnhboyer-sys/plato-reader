Dictionary (LSJ)

ἄκων (A), [ᾰ], οντος, ὁ, (ἀκή A) A. javelin, dart, smaller and lighter than ἔγχος, Il. 15.709, Od. 14.531, al., Pi. P. 9.20, E. Ph. 1402, etc.; in later Prose, Eratosth. Cat. 33, Aristid. Or. 26(14).84, Artem. 1.57, Ant.Lib. 41.5.
