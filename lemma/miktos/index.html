Dictionary (LSJ)

μικτός, ή, όν, A. mixed, blended, Ar. Th. 1114, Pl. Phlb. 22d, etc.; opp. ἁπλοῦς, Id. R. 547e; μ. ἐκ τούτων compounded of these, Id. Lg. 837b, cf. D.H. Dem. 41. Adv. -τῶς Str. 1.2.27, Gal. 9.703. 2. motley, POxy. 1153.14 (i A. D.). (In early texts μεικτός (which is written in PCair.Zen. 292.25, al. (iii B. C.)) shd. prob. be restored, v. μείγνυμι.)
