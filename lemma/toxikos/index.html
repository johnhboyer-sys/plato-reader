HP 4.11.11. 2. ἡ τοξική (sc. τέχνη) archery, Pl. Smp. 197a, La. 193b, al., SIG 1060.5 (Tralles, iv/iii B.C.). 3. τὸ -κόν shot-hole, loophole, LXXJd. 5.28, Sm. Ez. 40.16: so -κή (sc. θυρίς) Ph. Bel. 81.25. II. of persons, skilled in the use of the bow, [Πάνδαρος] Plu. 2.405b; τοξικώτατοι X. Cyr. 6.2.4. III. τὸ -κόν, collectively, the bowmen, for οἱ τοξόται, Ar.
