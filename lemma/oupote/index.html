οὔ ποτε or οὔποτε, Dor. οὔποκα Epich. 170, Call. Lav.Pall. 5,59: Adv.:—A. not ever, never, Hom. and Att. (Ar. Eq. 1289, Pl. Phdr. 245c, Lib. Ep. 1347.2, etc.): joined as well with fut. as with pres. and past tenses, Il. 1.234, 10.164, al.: sts. with one or more words between οὐ and ποτέ, Il. 1.163,4.48, etc.
