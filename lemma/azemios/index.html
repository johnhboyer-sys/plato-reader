Ra. 408, Antipho 3.3.10, etc.; ὑπὸ θεῶν Pl. R. 366a; not liable to penalty, ναῦς IG 1.40; not deserving punishment, S. El. 1102, etc.: c.gen., ἀσεβημάτων ἀ. Plb. 2.60.5.
