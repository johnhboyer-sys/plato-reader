[βίος] ib.22a; τὸ σ. the complex of both, Id. Smp. 209b, Ti. 87e, Epicur. Sent. 3. Gal. 6.237, Plot. 4.3.26, 6.9.2; or without the Art., Pl. R. 400c, Sph. 250c, Gal. 16.743; τοῦτο σ. this united power, D. 2.14. 3. Math., of the sum of two things, συναμφότερα τὰ Δ, M, . .
