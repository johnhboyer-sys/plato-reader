πάτριος, α, ον, Lyr. and Trag. ; also ος, ον E. Hel. 222, and Att. Com., Prose (exc. And. 3.27), and Inscrr. (so elsewh., SIG 539A 14 (Delph., iii B.C.), 558.21 (Ithaca, iii B.C.), etc.) : (πατήρ) :—A. of or belonging to oneʼs father, ἄρουρα Pi. O. 2.14 ; ὄσσα ib.6.62 ; γῆ S. Ant. 806 (lyr.), E. Med. 651 (lyr.) ; χθών Id. Hel. 222 ; τεύχεα, δώματα, S. Ph. 398 (lyr.), OT 1394.
