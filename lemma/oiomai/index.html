OC 28 ; but οἴομαι is freq. in Ar. (Eq. 407, al.) ; Hdt. does not use either form ; in Att. Prose codd. vary, but οἶμαι prevails, and was exclusively used in parenthesis (v. infr. IV) : impf. ᾠόμην A. Pr. 270, Ar. V. 791, etc. ; also 1 pers. ᾤμην Id. Fr. 636, etc. : fut. οἰήσομαι Lys. 30.8, Pl. R. 397a, etc., later οἰηθήσομαι Gal. Opt.Doctr. 42 :—Ep. aor. ὠϊσάμην (v. infr.) : aor. ὠΐσθην Od. 4.453, 16.475 ; part. ὀϊσθείς Il. 9.453 ; Att. and Ion. aor. ᾠήθην Hp. VM 14, Antipho 1.8, Th. 4.130, Pl. Tht. 178c, etc. ; but rare in Com. and Trag., οἰηθῇς Ar. Eq. 860, οἰηθείς, -εῖσα, Antiph. 194.2, E. IA 986 ; also aor. inf. οἰήσασθαι Arat. 896 :—Act., Ep. pres. ὀΐω and οἴω, but only in 1sg.
