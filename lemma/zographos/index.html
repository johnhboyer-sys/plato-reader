Dictionary (LSJ)

ζωγράφος, ὁ, (ζωός or ζωή, γράφω) A. one who paints from life or from nature, Hdt. 2.46, Pl. Grg. 448c, 453c, Lg. 656e, etc.: metaph., πολιτειῶν ζ. Id. R. 501c: generally, painter, Luc. Herod. 4, Epigr. 41. (ζωγρ- without iota, PSI 4.346, 407 (iii B.C.), SIG 682.3 (ii B.C.), Pap. in Abh.Berl.Akad. 1904(2).6(ii B.C.), EM 412.53: so ζωγραφία SIG 960.13 (ii B.C.), Phld. Rh. 2.166S.)
