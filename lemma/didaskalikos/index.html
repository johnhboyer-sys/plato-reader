Grg. 453e, cf. 455a: ἡ -κή (sc. τέχνη) the faculty of giving instruction, Id. Sph. 231b; τὸ -κόν Id. Lg. 813b; so, in disparagement, τὸ πρεσβυτικὸν καὶ δ. the didactic manner of old age, of Isocrates, Hermog. Id. 2.11: Comp. -ώτερος Arist. Metaph. 982a13. Adv. -κῶς Pl. Cra. 388c, Plb. 6.3.5: Comp. -ώτερον Dioph. 1p.474T., Hermog. Inv. 1.1. 2. -κή (sc. ὁμολογίἀ, ἡ, contract of apprenticeship, POxy. 275.34 (i A. D.). 3. Gramm., τόπος δ., locus classicus, Sch.Il. 5.857.
