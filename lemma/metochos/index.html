C.), Ostr.Bodl.i92, 251 (ii B. C.), Ev.Luc. 5.7, etc. 2. member of a board of officials, freq. in phrase ὁ δεῖνα καὶ μέτοχοι πράκτορες, ἐπιτηρηταί, ἀγορανόμοι, τραπεζῖται, etc., PFlor. 358.5 (ii A. D.), PSI 2.160.4 (ii A. D.), PStrassb. 52.17 (ii A. D.), POxy. 96.4 (ii A. D.), etc. 3. joint owner of a house, CPHerm. 119 Aiv 20 (iii A. D.). III. θεῶν μέτοχοι, of the demigods, Arist.
