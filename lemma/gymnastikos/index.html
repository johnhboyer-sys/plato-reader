Dictionary (LSJ)

γυμναστικός, ή, όν, A. fond of athletic exercises, skilled in them, Hp. Aph. 1.3; γ. ἢ ἰατρός Pl. Prt. 313d: Comp., Philostr. Gym. 35: Sup., ib.11; of the gymnastic master (opp. παιδοτρίβης, q.v.), Arist. Pol. 1288b18; γ. [θεραπεία] Pl. Grg. 464b: ἡ-κή (with or without τέχνη),gymnastics, Id. Smp. 187a, etc. Adv. -κῶς Ar. V. 1212. II. γ. λῆμμα (opp. ῥητορικόν) suited for dialectical discussion, Stoic. 2.76. Adv. -κῶς by means of testing, Simp. in Ph. 139.3.
