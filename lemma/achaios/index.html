Dictionary (LSJ)

Ἀχαιός, ά, όν, A. Achaean, Hom., etc.: hence as Subst., 1. Ἀχαιοί, οἱ, the Achaeans, in Hom. for the Greeks generally, Il. 2.235, etc. 2. Ἀχαΐα, ἡ, Achaia in Peloponnese, Th., etc.; under the Romans, the province of Greece.
