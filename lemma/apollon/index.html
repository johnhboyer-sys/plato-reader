Dictionary (LSJ)

Ἀπόλλων, ὁ, Α. Apollo: gen. ωνος (also ω An.Ox. 3.222): acc. Ἀπόλλω IG 1.9, al., A. Supp. 214, S. OC 1091, Tr. 209 (lyr.) (mostly in adjurations, νὴ τὸν Ἀπόλλω, etc.), Ἀπόλλωνα Pl. Lg. 624a, freq. later, Agatharch. 7, etc.: voc. Ἄπολλον Alc. 1, A. Th. 159(lyr.), Cratin. 186, etc.; Ἀπόλλων A. Ch. 559; cf. Ἀπέλλων, Ἄπλουν. II. Pythag. name of a number, Porph. Abst. 2.36.
