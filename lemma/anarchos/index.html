ἄναρχος, ον, A. without head or chief, Il. 2.703; ναυτικὸν στράτευμʼ ἄ. E. IA 914, cf. Hec. 607; ἄ. ζῷα, opp. τὰ ὑφʼ ἡγεμόνα ὄντα, Arist. HA 488a11: τὸ ἄ., = ἀναρχία, A Eu. 696. 2. ἔτος ἄ. a year without any regular magistrates, GDI 5635 (Teos). II. Act., holding no office or magistracy, prob. l. Arr. Epict. 4.6.3. b. not qualified to hold office, Max. Tyr. 21.5 (Sup., s. v. l.). III. without beginning, Parm. 8.27, Ocell. 1.2, S.E. M. 7.312; κύκλος ἄ. καὶ ἀτελεύτητος Procl. Inst. 146; ἄ. δίκη PLips. 33 ii 5 (iv A. D.). b. without first principles, S.E. M. 1.180.
