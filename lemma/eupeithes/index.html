Phdr. 271d; compliant, εὐ. γεγονέναι BGU 1104.23 (i B. C.), LXX 4 Ma. 12.6. etc.; of things, as of the voice, under control, Arist. Aud. 802a6; ὕλη (material) εἰς ἅπαν εὐ. Gal. 6.3; of food, Plu. 2.669b. Adv. -θῶς ib. 981a. II. Act., persuasive, of a rein, ἐϋπειθέϊ δεσμῷ Opp. C. 1.313; dub. l. in A. (v. εὐπιθής).
