Dictionary (LSJ)

ἑκατόν, Arc. ἑκοτόν IG 5(2).3 (Tegea, iv B.C.), οἱ, αἱ, τά, indecl.:— A. a hundred, Il. 2.510, etc.: in compds. freq. loosely for very many. (Dissim. from sém kṃtóm, cf. εἷς and Lat. centum, Lith. šiṁtas, etc.)
