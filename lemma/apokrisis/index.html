ἀπόκρισις, εως, ἡ, A. separation, Anaxag. 4; κάθαρσις ἀ. χειρόνων ἀπὸ βελτιόνων Pl. Def. 415d. 2. Medic., excretion or secretion, γονῆς Hp. Genit. 2, σιτίων Vict. 4.93, cf. Arist. HA 582a4, Pr. 878a23, etc.; ἀ.σπερματική, περιττωματική, PA 681b35; σπέρματος Epicur. Nat.Herc. 908.3. 3. ἀ. νοσηρή exhalation, miasma, Hp. Nat.Hom. 9. II. (from Med.) decision, answer, first in Thgn. 1167, cf. Hdt. 1.49, 5.50 codd. (ὑπόκρισις edd.), Hp. Decent. 3, Steril. 213, E. Fr. 977; ἀ. πρὸς τὸ ἐρώτημα Th. 3.60, cf. X. Hier. 1.35. 2. defence, Antipho 5.65. 3. rescript, Procop. Pers. 2.23; = responsum, Gloss. 4. embassy, commission, Chor. in Rev.Phil. 1.79. III. a kind of dance, Hsch.
