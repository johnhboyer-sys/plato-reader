ἁπτέον, (ἅπτομαι) A. one must cling to a thing, bestow pains upon it, μουσικῆς Pl. R. 377a, cf. Epicur. Fr. 461; one must partake of, πλακοῦντος Alex. 250, cf. Gal. 11.371, Porph. Abst. 2.44.
