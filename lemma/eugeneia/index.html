Euthd. 279b, R. 618d; pure breeding, of animals, Onos. 1.21. 2. = γενναιότης, nobleness of mind, Plu. Dem. 24, Ant. 86, Ael. VH 12.1, D.Chr. 52.16, etc. 3. bodily excellence, ἡ ἐν τοῖς σώμασιν εὐ. Plu. Rom. 6, cf. Gal. UP 10.6; of materials, χαλκοῦ Philostr. VA 3.54: generally, excellence, Lib. Or. 49.27. 4. of style, elevation, nobility, Longin. 34.2; ποιημάτων D.H.
