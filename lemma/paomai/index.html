*πάομαι, fut. πάσομαι [ᾱ] A. Eu. 177(lyr.); redupl. πεπάσομαι Pempel. ap. Stob. 4.25.52: aor. ἐπᾱσάμην Leg.Gort. 6.5, al., Thgn. 146, A. Fr. 215, Call. Cer. 128, Theoc. 15.90:—mostly Dor., Arc., and poet. (used by X., v. infr.), A. get, acquire, πᾱσάμενος ἐπίτασσε, i.e. order your own slaves, Theoc. l.c.: chiefly used in pf. πέπᾱμαι, = κέκτημαι, possess, Pi. P. 8.73, Fr. 105, E. Ion 675, Ar. Av. 943 (lyr.), Leg.Gort. 9.43, SIG 1164 (Dodona), Foed. Delph. Pell.2A 18, SIG 306.7 (Tegea, iv B. C.); 3pl. πέπανται X. An. 3.3.18; inf. πεπᾶσθαι Sol. 13.7, E. Andr. 641, Theoc. 10.32, Diotog. ap. Stob. 4.7.62; part. πεπᾱμένος A. Ag. 835, X. An. 6.1.12 (written πεπεμμένος in error, IG 42(1).77.18 (Epid., ii B. C.)): plpf. ἐπεπάμην X. An. 1.9.19 (dub. l.), AP 7.67 (Leon.).
